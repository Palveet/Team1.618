Team 1.618 is a team of students who are passionate about building the best open-wheel formula style race car.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design and fabricate hybrid vehicles which are efficient as well as economically feasible and environmentally acceptable. We aim to transform the current automobile generation into a greener and efficient one.

Our Values

We are engineers from diverse backgrounds from all over India with one motive - safer and greener future. As the future of this world is hybrid for safer and greener environment, we develop Hybrid vehicle prototypes and take part in the competition to provide the world with some good prototypes for the future.
