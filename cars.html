FORMULA PHI

FORMULA STUDENT

Institute of MECHANICAL ENGINEERS

Formula Student UK is the most prestigious engineering design competition for undergraduates. It is held annually at the renowned F1 circuit- Silverstone, Northamptonshire. Best universities from around the world participate in the event.

With the recent success of the team in the Hybrid Vehicle Challenge'18, Team 1.618 have undertaken the challenge to compete against the best.

Having qualified in the preliminary stages, the team is going to participate in Formula student UK in July'18 and in the upcoming years.

Research and Development of a race car is not a easy task, but with the determination and the motivation of our team memebers, we are sure to make India proud!
